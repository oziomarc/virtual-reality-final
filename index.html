THE

NIAGARA EXPERIENCE

The Niagara Gallery brings the traditional art gallery experience to virtual reality, granting audiences of various abilities, locations, and interests access to a sensational new world.

The Niagara Experience is the most interactive and immersive artistic escapade available, and promises to activate senses you never knew you had.

All from the comfort of your own home.

Using Unreal Engine's Niagara system, we have created nine unique, interactive exhibitions. Each piece has unique physics and interactions from fluids, to foliage, to audio visualization and more, but they all share the bright vibrance of our colorful gallery.

Niagara is your sandbox. Unlike most real-life and virtual immersive gallaries, the Niagara Experience gives you control of what you see, how you see it, and how you choose to interact with it. You call the shots here, and you can't go wrong.

This experience is not only ethereal and transformative, but you are a key actor. Can you figure out how to beat the bottleneck and 'break' into the gallery? Hint: how did you get past the white wall and into this website...
